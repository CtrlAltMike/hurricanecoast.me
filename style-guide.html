Ordered list

  1. Assess your household's specific needs.
  2. Gather supplies listed in your kit plan.
  3. Store in a cool, dark, accessible location.
  4. Review and rotate stock every 12 months.

Checklist — ochre bullet markers, .why note text in muted ink

  • One gallon of water per person per day — three days minimum per person.
  • Non-perishable food — shelf-stable, no cooking required.
  • A manual can opener — the one thing people forget every time.
  • Flashlight with extra batteries — one per person, not shared.
