Typography

Ready looks like care.

Newsreader 600–700 carries the masthead and headings. It should feel like a well-used reference book, not a campaign poster.

Plain language, comfortable measure.

Work Sans 400–700 carries guidance, navigation, forms, captions, Spanish, and data. Long-form prose is 18px/1.7 and stays within 60–68 characters per line.

Reading endurance

Decoration stops after the opening image.

Long-form guidance uses generous paper space, clear subheads, restrained rules, and short callouts only where the action truly changes. Not every idea becomes a card.

  • Lists keep their natural rhythm.
  • Captions sound observed and specific.
  • Printables remain monochrome and low-ink.
  • Motion is limited to a small hover response and the operational alert ticker.
