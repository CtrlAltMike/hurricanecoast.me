Evacuation kit

The bag by the door. You have twenty minutes. What goes in it, how much of it, and why, so that when the call comes, you're not thinking, you're moving.

Water and food (first 72 hours)

  • One gallon of water per person per day drinking plus a little for basic hygiene.
  • Three days of non-perishable food per person shelf-stable, no cooking required.
  • A manual can opener the one thing people forget every time.
