Wind study

Homepage wind directions

Every study below uses the real homepage hero proportions and content. The differences are preview-only motion treatments, meant to answer a narrower question: what does wind feel like here without cheapening the page?

I broadened this page away from just one headline-gust idea. The set now covers text-led, image-led, atmosphere-led, and mixed responses.

Idea 1

Headline pressure

Only the headline yields. The movement is a single-object shove, brief resistance on the right, and a long settle. Everything else stays planted.

Be ready before the storm.

Hurricanes are predictable in their season and manageable in their preparation. This site exists to help you build a kit, understand your risk, and know your options before you need any of it.
