Be ready before the storm.

Hurricanes are predictable in their season and manageable in their preparation. This site exists to help you build a kit, understand your risk, and know your options before you need any of it.

Preparedness isn't about fear.

It's about care. You prepare because you have people, pets, medications, documents, and routines worth protecting. A hurricane kit is not panic shopping. It's a set of decisions made early enough that landfall doesn't get to make them for you.

Most hurricane preparedness content online falls into one of two categories: government PDFs nobody reads, or affiliate roundups designed to sell you something. This site exists to do the middle thing well: practical guides that explain what to pack, how much, and why, written for real households on the Gulf and Atlantic coasts.

No ads. No affiliate links. Just specific guidance, printable checklists, and state pages you can send to someone before a storm is on the map.
