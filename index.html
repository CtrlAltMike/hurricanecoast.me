About this site

This site is built around one idea: preparedness is care, not fear. The goal of every guide is to give you specific, actionable information — what to buy, how much of it, and why — so that the preparation is done before it matters.

I'm not an emergency management professional. I'm someone who has done the research, lives in a region that takes this seriously, and wanted to make something genuinely useful. If it helps, consider leaving a tip.
